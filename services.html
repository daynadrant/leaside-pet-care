Services

Every pet is unique, and I'm here to meet their needs with calm, loving care.
In addition to feeding, fresh water, tidying litter, grooming, and playtime, I'm happy to accommodate special requests.
This can include things like quirks, medical needs, or customized routines - including oral or topical medication administration.

Neighbourhoods

I've worked mostly in the Leaside, Davisville Village, Rosedale, Lawrence Park, and Yonge areas, but I'm flexible with different neighbourhoods.

Pricing

My rate is $20-40 per day, depending on the number of in-home visits (once or twice daily) and your location.
Reach out for a personalized quote.

Pets

I mostly work with cats but have taken care of other small pets including hampsters, guinea pigs, and even the occasional fish.

Additional

Along with pet care, I'm happy to bring in mail, take out garbage, water plants, and handle any other house sitting tasks requested.
I'll keep you updated with photos and texts, so you can relax knowing everything's taken care of.
